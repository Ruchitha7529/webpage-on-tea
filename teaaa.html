Tea Is the Most Consumed Beverage in the World:

After water, tea is the most widely consumed beverage globally. It's an essential part of daily life for billions of people across all continents.

Tea Has Been Around for Thousands of Years:

The origins of tea trace back to ancient China, over 4,000 years ago. According to legend, Emperor Shen Nong discovered tea when a leaf blew into his cup of hot water.

Fun Facts About Tea

  • Tea is the second most consumed beverage in the world, after water.
  • China and India are the largest producers of tea.
  • Tea contains antioxidants that can help improve your health.
  • There are more than 1,500 different types of tea available.
